Cristiano Ronaldo (CR7)

The Icon of Football

Here's a timeline of CR7's life:

  • 6 February 1985 - Cristiano Ronaldo is born
  • 2 July 1993 - Ronaldo plays for his first amateur team
  • 2 June 1995 - Ronaldo signs with Nacional
  • 2 June 1997 - Sporting CP recruits Ronaldo
  • 8 October 2002 - Ronaldo makes his professional debut
  • 19 July 2003 - Ronaldo transfers to Manchester United
  • 17 August 2003 - Ronaldo makes his debut in the Premier League
  • 21 August 2003 - Ronaldo is called up for UEFA Euro 2004
  • 2 September 2005 - Jose, Ronaldo's father dies
  • 7 February 2007 - Ronaldo serves as Portugal's captain
  • 7 July 2009 - Ronaldo transfers to Real Madrid
  • 2 June 2010 - Ronaldo is represented as a waxwork
  • 18 June 2010 - Ronaldo has his first son
  • 25 March 2012 - Ronaldo scores his 100th league goal for Real Madrid
  • 2 December 2013 - Ronaldo opens a museum to house his trophies and other memorabilia
  • 6 March 2014 - Ronaldo is named as the Portuguese all-time top scorer
  • 2 January 2015 - Ronaldo is declared the most charitable sportsperson in the world
  • 13 April 2017 - Ronaldo becomes the first footballer to score 100 goals in the UEFA Club Competition
  • 11 July 2018 - Ronaldo signs a 4-year contract with Italian club Juventus
  • 27 July 2019 - Ronaldo wins his first trophy with Juventus
